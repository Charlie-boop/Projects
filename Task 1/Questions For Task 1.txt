Important Quantitative Questions:

What Country gets the most Sales?
What time of the Year do the most Sales Occur? 
What things sell the most?
What is the Lead to Customer Conversion Rate?
What is our Customer Acquisition Cost?
What is our Total New Customers?
What products get returned?
Who is our Qualified Lead? Do we depend on them or is our customer base diversified?


Important Qualitative Questions:
How do customers feel about our products and the service?
How do our products stack up against competitors?

How visible are we in the places where we sell the most/least?
